VORONOID
by Adam Scherlis About Voronoid

Maximize your territory!
Take turns placing your markers anywhere on the game field.
The field is carved up into the regions of a Voronoi diagram.
The value of each region is proportional to its area.

Divide and conquer!
Each region is worth an extra 50% for each neighboring ally.
(EG: A green region touching 4 other green ones is worth triple)
Try to divide your opponents' territory, but keep your own together!
